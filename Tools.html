TRACTOR

These are essential farming tools that can be used for plowing, planting, and harvesting. They come in different sizes and horsepower ratings to suit different needs.

Cultivator

Cultivators are used to prepare the soil for planting by breaking up soil clumps and removing weeds.

Seed Drill

These are machines used to plant seeds uniformly and at a predetermined depth. They can be rented for sowing crops like corn, wheat, and soybeans.

Sprayers

Sprayers are used to apply fertilizers and pesticides to crops. They come in different types, such as boom sprayers, handheld sprayers, and backpack sprayers.

Harvesters

Harvesters are used to harvest crops like wheat, maize, and soybeans. They come in different sizes and can be rented depending on the size of the farm and the amount of crops to be harvested.

Hay balers

These are used to collect and bale hay for storage and animal feed. Hay baler is a piece of farm machinery used to compress a cut and raked crop (such as hay, cotton, flax straw, salt marsh hay, or silage) into compact bales that are easy to handle, transport, and store.
